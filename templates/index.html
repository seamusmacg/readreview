About

ReadReview is site designed for readers and book recommendations. Our mission is to help people discover and share books they love. If you're looking for a place to find a great book or to share your opinion of a book you have read, you've found the right place!


This is a new site so the catalogue is still quite sparse so we encourage new visitors to this sign-up to the site and contribute to our community by updating the catalogue by adding books or by adding reviews to existing books.


Enough talk from us, please enjoy the site and more importantly get reading and reviewing!
